The Analyst's Toolkit

Debits, credits, journal entries — how inputs enter the system

We've examined the output of accounting systems: the financial statements. Now we look at the input side. Every transaction that affects an organization enters the accounting system through a journal entry, which uses the language of debits and credits. Understanding this toolkit is essential for analyzing transactions and understanding how data flows into the system.
