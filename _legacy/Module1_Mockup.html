How to Use This Prework

New to accounting?

Start at the beginning and take your time. Each module builds on the last, but you control the pace.

Some accounting background?

Take the diagnostic below to see where you can move quickly. But don't skip the framing — the information system perspective will be new, even if the mechanics are familiar.

For everyone

The quizzes are diagnostics, not tests. They don't count toward any grade. They exist to help you figure out where to spend your time.
