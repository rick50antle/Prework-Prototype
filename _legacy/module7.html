CVS Health: Cash Flows

Tracking what actually moved in and out

The income statement tells us what CVS Health earned; the balance sheet tells us what it owns. But neither directly answers the question: how much cash came in and went out? The cash flow statement bridges accrual accounting and reality, showing which transactions involved actual movement of money.
